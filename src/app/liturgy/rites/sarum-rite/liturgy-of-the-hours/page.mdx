## Matins

---

**Opening Versicles**
- [Domine labia mea](/liturgy/music/chants/domine-labia-mea) (Matins only)
- [Deus in adjutorium](/liturgy/music/chants/deus-in-adjutorium) w/ [Gloria Patri](/liturgy/music/chants/gloria-patri)

**Invitatory**
- [Venite](/liturgy/music/chants/venite)

**Hymn**
  - A hymn proper to the day is sung.

**1–3 Nocturnes**
- [Psalms](/liturgy/psalter#psalm-distribution) w/ antiphons
- Lessons w/ responsories

[**Te Deum**](/liturgy/music/chants/te-deum)
- **All Feasts and Lord's Days**
- **Daily During**
  - Christmastide
  - Octave of Epiphany
  - Octave of Easter
  - Whitsuntide
- **Omitted During**
  - Advent
  - Holy Innocents' Day, unless it falls on a Sunday
  - From Septuagesima to Passion Sunday (inclusive)
  - The Last Three Days of Holy Week
  - All Souls' Day[^1][^2]

## Lauds

---

- Opening Versicles
- Psalms w/ antiphons
- [Old Testament Canticle](/liturgy/psalter#old-testament-canticles)
- [Laudate Psalms](/glossary/liturgical-terms#laudate-psalms) (148, 149, 150)[^3]
- Revelation passage w/ responsory (Lord's Days)[^4]
- Epistle passage w/ responsory (Ferial Days)[^5]
- Hymn
- [Benedictus](/liturgy/music/chants/benedictus)
- Concluding Prayers[^6]
  - [Kyrie](/liturgy/music/chants/kyrie)
  - [Our Father](/liturgy/prayers/our-father)
  - [Suffrages](/glossary/liturgical-terms#suffrages)
  - [Salutation](/glossary/liturgical-terms#salutation)
  - [Collect](/glossary/liturgical-terms#collect)
- Closing Versicles[^7]
  - Salutation
  - [Benedicamus](/liturgy/music/chants/benedicamus)

## Prime

---

- Opening Versicles
- Hymn
- Psalms w/ antiphons
- [Athanasian Creed](/liturgy/creeds/athanasian-creed)
- Lesson w/ responsory
- Concluding Prayers
  - [Apostles' Creed](/liturgy/creeds/apostles-creed) before Suffrages
  - [Confiteor](/glossary/liturgical-terms#confiteor) before Collect
- Closing Versicles

## Terce–None

---

- Opening Versicles
- Hymn
- Psalms w/ antiphons
- Lesson w/ responsory
- Concluding Prayers
- Closing Versicles[^8]

## Vespers

---

- Opening Versicles
- Psalms w/ antiphons
- Lesson w/ responsory
- Hymn
- [Magnificat](/liturgy/music/chants/magnificat)
- Concluding Prayers
- Closing Versicles

## Compline

---

- Opening Versicles
- Psalms w/ antiphons
- Lesson w/ responsory
- Hymn
- [Nunc dimittis](/liturgy/music/chants/nunc-dimittis)
- Concluding Prayers
  - [Confiteor](/glossary/liturgical-terms#confiteor) before Collect
- Closing Versicles[^9]
