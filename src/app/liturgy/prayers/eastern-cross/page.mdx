#### Right Hand
- Christ sits at the right hand of God the Father.

#### Three Fingers
- The Holy Trinity

#### Two Fingers
- The two natures of Christ

#### Forehead
- *In the Name of the Father*
- He came from the Head, that is the Father.

#### Breast
- *And of the Son*
- He came down to us through his Incarnation.

#### Right Shoulder
- *And of the Holy*
- From his seat at the right hand of God the Father in glory.

#### Left Shoulder
- *Spirit.*
- He overcame the devil, who sits on the left in darkness (Matt. 25:33).

*Amen.*[^1]
