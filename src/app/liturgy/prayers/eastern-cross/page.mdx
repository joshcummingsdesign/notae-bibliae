#### Right Hand
- Jesus sits at the right hand of God the Father.

#### Three Fingers
- Represents the Holy Trinity.

#### Two Fingers
- Represents the two natures of Christ.

 ## Motion

<Image
  className="fixed-width"
  src="/images/sign-of-the-cross-right.png"

  width="189"

/>

#### Forehead
- "In the name of the Father..."

#### Breast
- "And of the Son..."
- The downward motion represents the Incarnation.

#### Right Shoulder
- "And of the Holy..."
- The shoulders represent power (Isa. 9:6).

#### Left Shoulder
- "Spirit."
- The left represents darkness (Matt. 25:33).
- The right-to-left motion represents Christ's overcoming of the devil.[^1]

[^1]: St. Peter of Damascus, [*A Treasury of Divine Knowledge*](https://orthodoxchurchfathers.com/fathers/philokalia/st-peter-of-damaskos-book-i-a-treasury-of-divine-knowledge.html), 1.40.7
