 ## Motion

#### Forehead
- "In the name of the Father..."
- The Father is the Head.
- The Father is the Principle without a principle.[^2]

#### Breast
- "And of the Son..."
- The downward motion represents the Incarnation.

#### Left Shoulder
- "And of the Holy..."
- The shoulders represent power (Isa. 9:6).
- The left represents darkness (Matt. 25:33).

#### Right Shoulder
- "Spirit."
- The left-to-right motion represents Christ’s Passion leading to his Ascension into heaven, where he is seated at the right hand of God the Father Almighty.[^3]
