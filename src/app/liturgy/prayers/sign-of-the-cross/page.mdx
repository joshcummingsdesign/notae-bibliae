- Christ sits at the right hand of God the Father.

#### Forehead
- *In the Name of the Father*
- He came from the Head, that is the Father.

#### Breast
- *And of the Son*
- He came down to us through his Incarnation.

#### Left Shoulder
- *And of the Holy*
- Darkness sits on the left (Matt. 25:33), whom he overcame through his Passion.

#### Right Shoulder
- *Spirit*.
- And ascended to the right hand of God the Father in glory.

*Amen.*[^1]

[^1]: [*The Myroure of Our Ladye*](https://archive.org/details/themyroureofoure00unkwuoft/page/n153/mode/2up), p. 80
