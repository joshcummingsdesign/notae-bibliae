## History

A [canticle](/glossary/liturgical-terms#canticle) for [Lauds](/glossary/liturgical-terms#lauds). The words come from the *Prayer of Azariah and Song of the Three Holy Children*, which is found in the Septuagint version of Daniel 3 and was included in the Vulgate.

The Latin setting below is from the *<Definition lang="latin" anchor="liber-usualis" text="Liber Usualis" />* (1961). The English setting below is from the *<Definition lang="english" anchor="saint-dunstans-psalter" text="Saint Dunstan's Psalter" />* (2002).
