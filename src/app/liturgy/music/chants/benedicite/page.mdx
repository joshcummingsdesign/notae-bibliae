## History

A canticle that is used in the [Liturgy of the Hours](/glossary/liturgical-terms#liturgy-of-the-hours). The Latin version was in existence by the 6th century, when it was prescribed for use at [Lauds](/glossary/liturgical-terms#lauds) in the [*Rule of Saint Benedict*](/people/saints/medieval/benedict-of-nursia). The words are based on The Prayer of Azariah and Song of the Three Holy Children, which is only found in the Septuagint version of Daniel 3, and was included in the Vulgate. The above setting is from the *Saint Dunstan's Plainsong Psalter* (2002), which makes use of the [Sarum Rite](/glossary/liturgical-terms#sarum-rite) tones.

## Text

**Benedicite, omnia opera Domini, Domino;**<br/>
**laudate et superexaltate eum in saecula.**<br/>
O all ye works of the Lord, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise him and magnify him for ever.

**Benedicite, angeli Domini, Domino.**<br/>
**Benedicite, caeli, Domino.**<br/>
O ye angles of the Lord, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.<br/>
O ye heavens, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.

**Benedicite, aquae omnes, quae super caelos sunt, Domino.**<br/>
**Benedicite omnes virtutes Domini, Domino.**<br/>
O ye waters that be above the firmament, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.<br/>
O all ye powers of the Lord, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.

**Benedicite, sol et luna, Domino.**<br/>
**Benedicite, stellae caeli, Domino.**<br/>
O ye sun and moon, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.<br/>
O stars of heaven, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.

**Benedicite, omnis imber et ros, Domino.**<br/>
**Benedicite, omnes venti, Domino.**<br/>
O ye showers and dew, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.<br/>
O ye winds of God, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.

**Benedicite, ignis et aestus, Domino.**<br/>
**Benedicite, frigus et aestus, Domino.**<br/>
O ye Fire and Heat, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.<br/>
O ye Winter and Summer, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.

**Benedicite, rores et pruina, Domino.**<br/>
**Benedicite, gelu et frigus, Domino.**<br/>
O ye dews and frosts, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.<br/>
O ye frost and cold, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.

**Benedicite, glacies et nives, Domino.**<br/>
**Benedicite, noctes et dies, Domino.**<br/>
O ye ice and snow, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.<br/>
O ye nights and days, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.

**Benedicite, lux et tenebrae, Domino.**<br/>
**Benedicite, fulgura et nubes, Domino.**<br/>
O ye light and darkness, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.<br/>
O ye lightnings and clouds, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.

**Benedicat terra Dominum;**<br/>
**laudet et superexaltet eum in saecula.**<br/>
O let the Earth bless the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.

**Benedicite, montes et colles, Domino.**<br/>
**Benedicite, universa germinantia in terra, Domino.**<br/>
O ye mountains and hills, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.<br/>
O all ye green things upon the earth, bless ye the Lord;
praise and magnify him forever.

**Benedicite, fontes, Domino.**<br/>
**Benedicite, maria et flumina, Domino.**<br/>
O ye wells, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.<br/>
O ye seas and floods, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.

**Benedicite, cete, et omnia, quae moventur in aquis, Domino.**<br/>
**Benedicite, omnes volucres caeli, Domino.**<br/>
O ye whales, and all that move in the waters, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.<br/>
O all ye fowls of the air, bless ye the Lord<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.

**Benedicite, omnes bestiae et pecora, Domino.**<br/>
**Benedicite, filii hominum, Domino.**<br/>
O all ye beasts and cattle, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.<br/>
O ye children of men, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.

**Benedic, Israel, Domino;**<br/>
**laudate et superexaltate eum in saecula.**<br/>
O let Israel bless the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.

**Benedicite, sacerdotes Domini, Domino.**<br/>
**Benedicite, servi Domini, Domino.**<br/>
O ye priests of the Lord, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.<br/>
O ye servants of the Lord, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.

**Benedicite, spiritus et animae iustorum, Domino.**<br/>
**Benedicite, sancti et humiles corde, Domino.**<br/>
O ye spirits and souls of the righteous, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.<br/>
O ye holy and humble men of heart, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.

**Benedicite, Anania, Azaria, Misael, Domino;**<br/>
**laudate et superexaltate eum in saecula.**<br/>
O Ananias, Azarias and Misael, bless ye the Lord;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.

**Benedicamus Patrem et Filium cum Sancto Spiritu;**<br/>
**laudemus et superexaltemus eum in saecula.**<br/>
Let us bless the Father, and the Son, and the Holy Ghost;<br/>
praise and magnify him forever.
