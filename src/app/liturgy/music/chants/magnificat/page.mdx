## History

The <Definition lang="latin" anchor="magnificat" text="Magnificat" /> is a Gospel [canticle](/glossary/liturgical-terms#canticle) for [Vespers](/glossary/liturgical-terms#vespers). The words come from Luke 1:46-55, St. Mary's prayer of thanksgiving at her Visitation to her cousin Elizabeth. The Magnificat, [Benedictus](/liturgy/music/chants/benedictus), and [Nunc dimittis](/liturgy/music/chants/nunc-dimittis) form the first three canticles in the Gospel of Luke.
