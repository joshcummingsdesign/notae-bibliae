## History

This is an antiphon for use at [Compline](/glossary/liturgical-terms#compline) in the Liturgy of the Hours. It is an evening prayer which traces back to as early as the 6th century. In 1961, French Benedictine monks at Solesmes Abbey (northwest France) published a melody for this chant in the *<Definition lang="latin" anchor="liber-usualis" text="Liber Usualis" />*.

## Text

**Salva nos, Dómine, vigilántes,**<br/>
Save us, Lord, keeping watch,

**custódi nos dormiéntes,**<br/>
guard us sleeping,

**ut vigilémus cum Christo**<br/>
so that we may keep watch with Christ,

**et requiescámus in pace.**<br/>
and so that we may rest in peace.
