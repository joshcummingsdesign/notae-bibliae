## Text

<Poetry>
|  \b Salva nos, Domine, vigilantes,
|  \b custodi nos dormientes:
|  \b ut vigilemus cum Christo,
|  \b et requiescamus in pace.
|
|  Save us, Lord, keeping watch,
|  guard us sleeping,
|  so that we may keep watch with Christ,
|  and so that we may rest in peace.
</Poetry>
