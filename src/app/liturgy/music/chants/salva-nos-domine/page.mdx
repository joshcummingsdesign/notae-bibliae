## History

An [antiphon](/glossary/liturgical-terms#antiphon) which frames the [Nunc dimittis](/liturgy/music/chants/nunc-dimittis) at [Compline](/glossary/liturgical-terms#compline) on Lord's Days.

## Text

<Poetry>
|  \b Salva nos, Domine, vigilantes,
|  \b custodi nos dormientes:
|  \b ut vigilemus cum Christo,
|  \b et requiescamus in pace. % Alleluia.[^2]
|
|  Save us, O Lord, while we are awake,[^3]
|  guard us while we sleep:
|  that we may watch with Christ,
|  and rest in peace. % Alleluia.
</Poetry>

[^1]: "Salva nos Domine", *<Definition lang="latin" anchor="liber-usualis" text="Liber Usualis" />*, 271–272
[^2]: <Red text="‡" /> The Alleluia is only sung during Eastertide.
[^3]: Or "Keep us, O Lord, while we watch"
