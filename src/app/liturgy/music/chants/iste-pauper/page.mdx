## History

This antiphon comes from Psalm 34:6. It has been heard chanted to the melody of [Gloria Patri II](/liturgy/music/chants/gloria-patri), which was published in 1961 by French Benedictine monks at Solesmes Abbey (northwest France) in the *<Definition lang="latin" anchor="liber-usualis" text="Liber Usualis" />*.

## Text

***Iste pauper clamavit et Dominus exaudivit eum,***<br/>
This poor man cried, and the Lord heard him,

***et de omnibus tribulationibus eius salvavit eum***.<br/>
and saved him out of all his troubles.
