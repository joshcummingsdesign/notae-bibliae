## Text

<Poetry>
|  \b Iste pauper clamavit
|  \b et Dominus exaudivit eum,
|  \b et de omnibus tribulationibus eius
|  \b salvavit eum.
|
|  This poor man cried,
|  and the Lord heard him,
|  and out of all his troubles
|  saved him.
</Poetry>
