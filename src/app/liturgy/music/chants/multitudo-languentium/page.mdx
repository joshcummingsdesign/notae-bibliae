## Text

<Poetry>
|  \b Multitudo languentium,
|  \b et qui vexabantur
|  \b a spiritibus immundis,
|  \b veniebant ad eum:
|  \b quia virtus de illo exibat,
|  \b et sanabat omnes.
|
|  A multitude of the sick,
|  and those troubled
|  by unclean spirits,
|  came to him:
|  for virtue went out from him
|  and healed them all.
</Poetry>
