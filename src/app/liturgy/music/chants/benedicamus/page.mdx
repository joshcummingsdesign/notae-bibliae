## History

The <Definition lang="latin" anchor="benedicamus-domino" text="Benedicamus Domino" />, or "Benedicamus" for short, is a [versicle](/glossary/liturgical-terms#versicle) which has been sung at the dismissal of Mass on fasting or penitential days since around the 11th century.[^1] It is also sung as a closing versicle in the Liturgy of the Hours.[^2]
