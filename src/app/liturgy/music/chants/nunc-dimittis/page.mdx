## History

The <Definition lang="latin" anchor="nunc-dimittis" text="Nunc dimittis" /> is a Gospel [canticle](/glossary/liturgical-terms#canticle) for [Compline](/glossary/liturgical-terms#compline). The words come from Luke 2:29–32, Simeon's blessing to the baby Jesus when he was presented at the temple in Jerusalem. The [Magnificat](/liturgy/music/chants/magnificat), [Benedictus](/liturgy/music/chants/benedictus), and Nunc dimittis form the first three canticles in the Gospel of Luke.
