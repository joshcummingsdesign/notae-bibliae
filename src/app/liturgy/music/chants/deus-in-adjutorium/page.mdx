## History

*Deus in adjutorium* is a versicle based on Psalm 70:1. In the 6th century it was prescribed for use at [Matins](/glossary/liturgical-terms#matins) in the [*Rule of Saint Benedict*](/people/saints/medieval/benedict-of-nursia). The setting below is from the *<Definition lang="latin" anchor="antiphonale-romanum" text="Antiphonale Romanum" />* (1912).

## Text

***Deus in adjutorium meum intende.***<br/>
O God, make speed to save me.

***Domine ad adjuvandum me festina.***<br/>
O Lord, make haste to help me.
