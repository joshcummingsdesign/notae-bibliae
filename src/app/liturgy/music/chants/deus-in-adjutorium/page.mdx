## History

*Deus in Adjutorium* is a versicle based on Psalm 70:1. In the 6th century it was prescribed for use at Matins in the *Rule of Saint Benedict*. The 1912 *Antiphonale Romanum* (Latin: "Roman Antiphonal") sets it to the common tone called "*Tonus festivus*" (Latin: "Festive tone").

## Text

***Deus in adjutorium meum intende.***<br/>
O God, come to my aid.

***Domine ad adjuvandum me festina.***<br/>
O Lord, make haste to help me.
