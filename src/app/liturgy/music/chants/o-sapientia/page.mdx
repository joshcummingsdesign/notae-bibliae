## History

The first of the [O Antiphons](/glossary/liturgical-terms#o-antiphons), which dates back to the 6th century. The focus is the Lord as Wisdom (Isa. 11:2) or in Latin *<Definition lang="latin" anchor="sapientia" text="Sapientia" />*.

## Text

<Poetry>
|  \b O Sapientia, quae ex ore Altissimi prodiisti,
|  \b attingens a fine usque ad finem,
|  \b fortiter suaviter disponensque omnia:
|  \b veni ad docendum nos viam prudentiae.
|
|  **Literal**
|  O Wisdom, coming forth from the mouth of the Most High,
|  reaching from one end to the other,
|  mightily and sweetly ordering all things:
|  come and teach us the way of prudence.
</Poetry>
