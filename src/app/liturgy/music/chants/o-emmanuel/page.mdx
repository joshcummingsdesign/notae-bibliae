## History

The seventh of the [O Antiphons](/glossary/liturgical-terms#o-antiphons) for December 23rd, which dates back to the 6th century. The focus is the Lord as "God with us" (Isa. 7:14), or in Hebrew *<Definition lang="hebrew" anchor="immanuel" text="Immanuel" />*.

## Text

<Poetry>
|  \b O Emmanuel, Rex et legifer noster,
|  \b exspectatio gentium et Salvator earum:
|  \b veni ad salvandum nos, Domine Deus noster.
|
|  O Emmanuel, our King and our lawgiver,
|  the hope of the nations and their Savior:
|  come and save us, O Lord our God.
</Poetry>
