## History

The seventh of the [O Antiphons](/glossary/liturgical-terms#o-antiphons) for December 23rd, which dates back to the 6th century. The focus is the Lord as "God with us" (Isa. 7:14), or in Hebrew *<Definition lang="hebrew" anchor="immanuel" text="Immanuel" />*. In 1961, French Benedictine monks at Solesmes Abbey (northwest France) published a melody for this chant in the *<Definition lang="latin" anchor="liber-usualis" text="Liber Usualis" />*.

## Text

***O Emmanuel, Rex et legifer noster,***<br/>
O Emmanuel, our King and our lawgiver,<br/>
***exspectatio gentium et Salvator earum:***<br/>
the hope of the nations and their Saviour:<br/>
***veni ad salvandum nos, Domine Deus noster.***<br/>
come and save us, O Lord our God.
