## History

An antiphon for [Lauds](/glossary/liturgical-terms#lauds) during Christmastide. The text comes from Luke 2:13-14, where the angels appear to the shepherds.

The setting below is from the *<Definition lang="latin" anchor="liber-usualis" text="Liber Usualis" />* (1961).

## Text

<Poetry>
|  \b Facta est cum Angelo
|  \b multitudo caelestis exercitus
|  \b laudantium et dicentium:
|  \b Gloria in excelsis Deo,
|  \b et in terra pax hominibus
|  \b bonae voluntatis. Alleluia.
|
|  There was with the Angel
|  a multitude of the heavenly host
|  praising and saying:
|  Glory to God in the highest,
|  and on earth peace to men
|  of good will. Alleluia.
</Poetry>
