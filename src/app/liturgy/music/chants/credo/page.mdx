## History

The <Definition lang="latin" anchor="credo" text="Credo" /> is the portion of the Mass where the [Nicene Creed](/liturgy/creeds/nicene-creed-with-filioque) is recited or sung. Along with the [Kryie](/liturgy/music/chants/kyrie), [Gloria](/liturgy/music/chants/gloria), [Sanctus](/liturgy/music/chants/sanctus), and [Agnus Dei](/liturgy/music/chants/agnus-dei), it is part of the Ordinary of the Mass. In 1896, French Benedictine monks at Solesmes Abbey (northwest France) published several chant melodies for the Credo in the *<Definition lang="latin" anchor="liber-usualis" text="Liber Usualis" />*, e.g. Credo III.

## Text

***Credo in unum Deum,***<br/>
I believe in one God,<br/>
***Patrem omnipotentem,***<br/>
the Father Almighty,<br/>
***Factorem caeli et terrae,***<br/>
Maker of heaven and earth,<br/>
***visibilium omnium et invisibilium;***<br/>
of all things visible and invisible;

***Et in unum Dominum Iesum Christum,***<br/>
And in one Lord Jesus Christ,<br/>
***Filium Dei unigenitum,***<br/>
the only Son of God,<br/>
***et ex Patre natum, ante omnia saecula,***<br/>
and begotten of the Father, before all ages,<br/>
***Deum de Deo, Lumen de Lumine,***<br/>
God from God, Light from Light,<br/>
***Deum verum de Deo vero,***<br/>
true God from true God,<br/>
***genitum, non factum, consubstantialem Patri;***<br/>
begotten, not made, consubstantial with the Father;<br/>
***Per quem omnia facta sunt.***<br/>
Through him all things were made.<br/>
***Qui propter nos homines et propter nostram salutem descendit de caelis,***<br/>
For us men and for our salvation he came down from heaven,<br/>
***et incarnatus est de Spiritu Sancto***<br/>
and by the Holy Spirit was incarnate<br/>
***ex Maria Virgine, et homo factus est.***<br/>
of the Virgin Mary, and became man.<br/>
***Crucifixus etiam pro nobis sub Pontio Pilato,***<br/>
For our sake he was crucified under Pontius Pilate,<br/>
***passus et sepultus est,***<br/>
suffered [death] and was buried,<br/>
***et resurrexit tertia die, secundum Scripturas,***<br/>
and rose again on the third day, in accordance with the Scriptures,<br/>
***et ascendit in caelum; sedet ad dexteram Patris.***<br/>
and ascended into heaven; he sits at the right hand of the Father.<br/>
***Et iterum venturus est cum gloria***<br/>
He will come again in glory<br/>
***iudicare vivos et mortuos,***<br/>
to judge the living and the dead,<br/>
***cuius regni non erit finis.***<br/>
and his kingdom will have no end.

***Et in Spiritum Sanctum, Dominum et vivificantem,***<br/>
I believe in the Holy Spirit, the Lord and the Giver of life,<br/>
***qui ex Patre Filioque procedit,***<br/>
who proceeds from the Father and the Son,<br/>
***qui cum Patre et Filio simul adoratur et conglorificatur,***<br/>
who with the Father and the Son is adored and glorified,<br/>
***qui locutus est per prophetas.***<br/>
who has spoken through the prophets.

***Et unam, sanctam, catholicam et apostolicam ecclesiam;***<br/>
I believe in one holy, catholic and apostolic church;<br/>
***confiteor unum baptisma in remissionem peccatorum;***<br/>
I confess one baptism for the remission of sins;<br/>
***et exspecto resurrectionem mortuorum,***<br/>
and I look forward to the resurrection of the dead,<br/>
***et vitam venturi saeculi. Amen.***<br/>
and the life of the world to come. Amen.
