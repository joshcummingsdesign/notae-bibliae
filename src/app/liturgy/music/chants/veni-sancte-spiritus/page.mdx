## Text

<Poetry>
|  \b Veni Sancte Spiritus,
|  \b Et emitte caelitus
|  \b Lucis tuae radium.
|
|  Come Holy Spirit,
|  And send out from heaven
|  The ray of your light.
|
|  \b Veni pater pauperum,
|  \b Veni dator munerum,
|  \b Veni lumen cordium.
|
|  Come father of the poor,
|  Come giver of gifts,
|  Come light of hearts.
|
|  \b Consolator optime,
|  \b Dulcis hospes animae,
|  \b Dulce refrigerium.
|
|  Greatest comforter,
|  Sweet guest of the soul,
|  Sweet consolation.
|
|  \b In labore requies,
|  \b In aestu temperies,
|  \b In fletu solatium.
|
|  In labour rest,
|  In heat temperateness,
|  In tears solace.
|
|  \b O lux beatissima,
|  \b Reple cordis intima
|  \b Tuorum fidelium.
|
|  O most blessed light,
|  Fill the inmost heart
|  Of your faithful.
|
|  \b Sine tuo numine,
|  \b Nihil est in homine,
|  \b Nihil est innoxium.
|
|  Without the nod of your head,
|  There is nothing in man,
|  Nothing that is harmless.
|
|  \b Lava quod est sordidum,
|  \b Riga quod est aridum,
|  \b Sana quod est saucium.
|
|  Cleanse what is unclean,
|  Water what is parched,
|  Heal what is wounded.
|
|  \b Flecte quod est rigidum,
|  \b Fove quod est frigidum,
|  \b Rege quod est devium.
|
|  Bend what is rigid,
|  Warm what is cold,
|  Guide what has deviated from the path.
|
|  \b Da tuis fidelibus,
|  \b In te confidentibus,
|  \b Sacrum septenarium.
|
|  Give to your faithful,
|  Who trust in you,
|  The sevenfold gift.
|
|  \b Da virtutis meritum,
|  \b Da salutis exitum,
|  \b Da perenne gaudium. Amen. Alleluia.
|
|  Give virtue's reward,
|  give salvation's end,
|  give joy eternal. Amen. Alleluia.
</Poetry>
