## History

*Domine labia mea* is a versicle based on Psalm 51:15. In the 6th century it was prescribed for use at [Matins](/glossary/liturgical-terms#matins) in the [*Rule of Saint Benedict*](/people/saints/medieval/benedict-of-nursia). The setting below is from the *<Definition lang="latin" anchor="nocturnale-romanum" text="Nocturnale Romanum" />* (2002).

## Text

***Domine labia mea aperies,***<br/>
O Lord, open thou my lips,

***et os meum adnuntiabit laudem tuam.***<br/>
and my mouth shall show forth thy praise.
