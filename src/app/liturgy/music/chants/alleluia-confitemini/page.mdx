## History

An [antiphon](/glossary/liturgical-terms#antiphon) from Psalm 118:1-2 with Alleluia for Eastertide.

The setting below is a transcription of Benedictine monks at Solesmes Abbey in northwestern France.

## Text

<Poetry>
|  \b Alleluia, confitemini Domino quoniam _ bonus: *
|  \b quoniam in saeculum misericor_dia ejus.
|
|  Alleluia, give praise to the Lord, for he is good:
|  for his mercy endureth forever.
|
|  \b Alleluia, dicat nunc Israel quoniam _ bonus: *
|  \b quoniam in saeculum misericor_dia ejus.
|
|  Alleluia, let Israel now say that he is good:
|  that his mercy endureth forever.
</Poetry>
