## Text

<Poetry>
|  \b 1. Confitemini Domino quoniam _ bonus: *
|  \b quoniam in saeculum misericor_dia ejus.
|
|  Give praise to the Lord, for he is good:
|  for his mercy endureth forever.
|
|  \b 2. Dicat nunc Israel quoniam _ bonus: *
|  \b quoniam in saeculum misericor_dia ejus.
|
|  Let Israel now say that he is good:
|  that his mercy endureth forever.
</Poetry>

[^1]: Amadeo Santiago Muñoz, "Alleluia con salmo 117", Verbum Gloriae, accessed October 13, 2025, https://www.verbumgloriae.es/project/alleluia-salmo-117/, Transcription of Benedictine nuns at Solesmes Abbey in northwestern France.
