## History

This Alleluia comes from Psalm 118:1-2. A beautiful melody for this chant comes from French Benedictine monks at Solesmes Abbey (northwest France).

## Text

Alleluia x18

***Confitemini Domino quoniam bonus quoniam in saeculum misericordia ejus.***<br/>
Give praise to the Lord, for he is good: for his mercy endureth for ever.

Alleluia x9

***Dicat nunc Israhel quoniam bonus quoniam in saeculum misericordia ejus.***<br/>
Let Israel now say, that he is good: that his mercy endureth for ever.
