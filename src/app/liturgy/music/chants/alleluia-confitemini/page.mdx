## History

An antiphon from Psalm 118:1 with Alleluia for [Prime](/glossary/liturgical-terms#prime) in the Liturgy of the Hours. The above setting is a transcription of Benedictine monks at Solesmes Abbey in northwest France.

## Text

**Alleluia.**<br/>
Alleluia.<br/>

***Confitemini Domino quoniam bonus;**<br/>
**quoniam in saeculum misericordia ejus.***<br/>
Give praise to the Lord, for he is good;<br/>
for his mercy endures forever!
