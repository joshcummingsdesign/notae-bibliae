## Text

<Poetry>
|  \b Victimae paschali laudes
|  \b immolent Christiani.
|
|  Let Christians offer sacrificial
|  praises to the passover victim.
|
|  \b Agnus redemit oves:
|  \b Christus innocens Patri
|  \b reconciliavit peccatores.
|
|  The Lamb has redeemed the sheep:
|  The Innocent Christ has reconciled
|  sinners to the Father.
|
|  \b Mors et vita duello
|  \b conflixere mirando:
|  \b dux vitae mortuus,
|  \b regnat vivus.
|
|  Death and life contended
|  in a spectacular battle:
|  the dead leader of life,
|  reigns alive.
|
|  \b Dic nobis Maria,
|  \b quid vidisti in via?
|
|  Tell us, Mary,
|  what did you see on the way?
|
|  \b Sepulcrum Christi viventis,
|  \b et gloriam vidi resurgentis:
|
|  I saw the tomb of the living Christ,
|  and the glory of his rising:
|
|  \b Angelicos testes,
|  \b sudarium, et vestes.
|
|  The angelic witnesses,
|  the shroud, and the clothes.
|
|  \b Surrexit Christus spes mea:
|  \b praecedet suos in Galilaeam.
|
|  Christ my hope is arisen:
|  he will go before his own into Galilee.
|
|  \b Scimus Christum surrexisse
|  \b a mortuis vere:
|  \b tu nobis, victor Rex, miserere. Amen. Alleluia.
|
|  We know Christ is truly risen
|  from the dead:
|  On us, O conqueror, King, have mercy. Amen. Alleluia.
</Poetry>
