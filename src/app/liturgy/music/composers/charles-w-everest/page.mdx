Charles W. Everest (1814–1877) was born in East Windsor, Connecticut, graduated from Trinity College in Hartford, and became rector of Grace Episcopal Church in Hamden. He is most famous for writing the hymn ["Take Up Thy Cross, The Savior Said"](/liturgy/music/hymns/take-up-thy-cross-the-savior-said).
