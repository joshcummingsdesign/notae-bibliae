Jill Jackson-Miller (1913–1995) was an American actress famous for writing the song ["Let There Be Peace on Earth"](/liturgy/music/hymns/let-there-be-peace-on-earth) with her husband [Sy Miller](/liturgy/music/composers/sy-miller).
