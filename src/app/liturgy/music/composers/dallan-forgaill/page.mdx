<Initial text="Dallán Forgaill" />

St. Dallán Forgaill (560–640) was a 6th-century Irish poet and martyr. The famous hymn ["Be Thou My Vision"](/liturgy/music/hymns/be-thou-my-vision) is attributed to him.
