<Initial text="Edmond Louis Budry" />

Edmond Louis Budry (1854–1932) was a Swiss pastor of the Free Church in Vevey. Besides writing original hymns, he translated German, English, and Latin lyrics into French. His most notable hymn is ["Thine Be the Glory"](/liturgy/music/hymns/thine-be-the-glory).
