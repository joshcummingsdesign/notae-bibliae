Folliott Sandford Pierpoint (1835–1917) was a high-church Anglican schoolmaster who was born in Bath, England (southwest England) and educated at Queens' College, Cambridge. He was involved in the Oxford Movement, and is most famous for writing the Eucharistic hymn ["For the Beauty of the Earth"](/liturgy/music/hymns/for-the-beauty-of-the-earth).
