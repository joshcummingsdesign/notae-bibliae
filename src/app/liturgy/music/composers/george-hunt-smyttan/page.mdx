George Hunt Smyttan (1822–1870) was an English Anglican priest and hymnist. He studied at Corpus Christi College in Cambridge, England, and graduated in 1845. He was ordained and Anglican deacon in 1848, priest in 1849, and appointed rector of Hawksworth, Nottinghamshire in 1850. He is most known for writing the hymn ["40 Days and 40 Nights"](/liturgy/music/hymns/40-days-and-40-nights).
