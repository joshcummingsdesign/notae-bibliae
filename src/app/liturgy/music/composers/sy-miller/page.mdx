Sy Miller (1908–1971) was an American songwriter famous for writing the song ["Let There Be Peace on Earth"](/liturgy/music/hymns/let-there-be-peace-on-earth) with his wife [Jill Jackson-Miller](/liturgy/music/composers/jill-jackson-miller).
