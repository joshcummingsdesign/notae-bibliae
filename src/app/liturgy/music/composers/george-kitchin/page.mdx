George Kitchin (1827–1912) was an English Anglican priest. In 1883, he was appointed the Dean of Winchester (southern England), and in 1894, he was appointed the Dean of Durham (northeastern England). He was made Chancellor of the University of Durham in 1910. He is most famous for writing ["Lift High the Cross"](/liturgy/music/hymns/lift-high-the-cross).
