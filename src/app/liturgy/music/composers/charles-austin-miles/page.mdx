<Initial text="Charles Austin Miles" />

Charles Austin Miles (1868–1946) was an American pharmacist who quit his job to be a hymnist. He worked for Hall-Mack Publishing Company and wrote over 400 hymns, the most famous of which is ["In the Garden"](/liturgy/music/hymns/in-the-garden).
