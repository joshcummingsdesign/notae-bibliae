<Initial text="Ernest W. Blandy" />

Ernest W. Blandy was born in 1849. He was a British minister that migrated to the USA in 1884 with his wife, Eliza. He became an officer in the Salvation Army and, in 1890, felt called to live in a Manhattan New York slum called "Hell's Kitchen". He is most famous for writing the hymn ["Where He Leads Me"](/liturgy/music/hymns/where-he-leads-me).
