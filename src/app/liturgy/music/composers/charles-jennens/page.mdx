<Initial text="Charles Jennens" />

Charles Jennens (1700–1773) was an English Anglican nobleman and dear friend of [George Frideric Handel](/liturgy/music/composers/george-frideric-handel). Jennens is most famous for writing the libretto (text of an opera or other long vocal work) to [Handel's Messiah](/liturgy/music/songs/handels-messiah).
