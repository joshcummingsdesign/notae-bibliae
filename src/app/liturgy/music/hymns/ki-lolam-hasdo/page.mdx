## History

The words to the hymn *<Definition lang="hebrew" anchor="ki-lolam-hasdo" text="Ki L'olam Haso" />* come directly from Psalm 118:29. Psalm 118 is the last of the <Definition lang="hebrew" anchor="hallel" text="Egyptian Hallel Psalms" /> traditionally sung at Passover. The melody was composed by Deborah Sacks Mintz.
