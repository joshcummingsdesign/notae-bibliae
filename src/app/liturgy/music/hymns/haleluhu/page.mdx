## History

The words to the hymn *<Definition lang="hebrew" anchor="halleluhu" text='"Haleluhu"' />* come directly from Psalm 150:5-6. The hymn is commonly sung to the tune of an old Jewish folk song.

## Text

**הַלְלוּהוּ בְּצִלְצְלֵי שָׁמַע**<br/>
[Haleluhu, haleluhu b'tzil-tz'lay shama]<br/>
Praise him with sounding cymbals

**הַלְלוּהוּ בְּצִלְצְלֵי תְרוּעָה**<br/>
[Haleluhu, haleluhu b'tzil-tz'lay t'ruah]<br/>
Praise him with loud clashing cymbals

**כֹּל הַנְּשָׁמָה תְּהַלֵּל יָהּ**<br/>
[Kol han'shama t'hallel yah]<br/>
Let everything that has breath praise the Lord

**הַלְלוּיָהּ**<br/>
[Haleluya, haleluya]<br/>
Praise the Lord
