Septuagesima – Lent

## Significance

<Definition lang="english" anchor="lenten" text="Pre-Lent" /> is the season encompassing the period of time from Septuagesima leading up to [Lent](/liturgy/seasons/lent). It traditionally opens a period of religious instruction leading to the reception of catechumens at Easter. The season focuses on examination of conscience and repentance before the Lenten fast.
