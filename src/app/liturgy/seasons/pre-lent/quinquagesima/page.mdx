## Significance

<Definition lang="latin" anchor="quinquagesima" text="Quinquagesima" /> comes from the Latin for 50th, as it falls around 50 days before Easter. It is observed on the Sunday before Lent. It is a time marked by spiritual preparation for the season of Lent.
