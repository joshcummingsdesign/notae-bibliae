## Significance

<Definition lang="latin" anchor="sexagesima" text="Sexagesima" /> comes from the Latin for 60th, as it falls around 60 days before Easter. It is observed on the Second Sunday before Lent. It is a time marked by spiritual preparation for the season of Lent.
