## Significance

<Definition lang="latin" anchor="septuagesima" text="Septuagesima" /> comes from the Latin for 70th, as it falls around 70 days before Easter. It is observed on the Third Sunday before Lent. It is a time marked by spiritual preparation for the season of Lent.
