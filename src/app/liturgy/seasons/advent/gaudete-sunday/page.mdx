## Significance

<Definition lang="latin" anchor="gaudete" text="Gaudete Sunday" /> is the Third Sunday of Advent, and is a pause in the penitential spirit of Advent. As the name implies, Gaudete Sunday deals with rejoicing in the Lord as well as remembering the mission of [St. John the Baptist](/people/saints/biblical/john-the-baptist) and his connection with Advent.
