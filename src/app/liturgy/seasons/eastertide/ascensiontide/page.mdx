Ascension Day – Seventh Sunday of Easter

## Significance

Ascensiontide (Ascension + -<Definition lang="english" anchor="tide" text="tide" />) is the [octave](/glossary/liturgical-terms#octave) which begins on Ascension Day and ends on the following Sunday, the Seventh Sunday of Easter.
