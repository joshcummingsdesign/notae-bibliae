## Easter Vigil

1. Service of the Light
   - The <Definition lang="latin" anchor="paschalis" text="Paschal candle" /> is lit, symbolizing the Light of Christ.

2. The Liturgy of the Word
   - Seven Old Testament Readings
     1. Gen. 1:1-31, Gen. 2:1-2, Ps. 104:1-35
     2. Gen. 22:2-18, Ps. 16:1-11
     3. Ex. 14:15-31, Ex. 15:1-18
     4. Isa. 54:5-14, Ps. 30:1-12
     5. Isa. 55:1-11, Isa. 12:2-6
     6. Bar. 3:9-15, Bar. 3:32-38, Bar. 4:1-4, Ps. 19:1-14
     7. Ez. 36:16-28, Ps. 42:1-11

   - Two New Testament Readings
     - Rom. 6:3-11, Ps. 118:1-29
     - Mark 16:1-7
