**October 31 – November 2**

Allhallowtide (<Definition lang="english" anchor="hallow" text="All Hallows'" /> + -<Definition lang="english" anchor="tide" text="tide" />) is the three-day period including All Hallows' Eve, All Saints' Day, and All Souls' Day.

## Observances

* **October 31** — [All Hallows' Eve](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/trinitytide/allhallowtide/all-hallows-eve)
* **November 1** — [All Saints' Day](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/trinitytide/allhallowtide/all-saints-day)
* **November 2** — [All Souls' Day](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/trinitytide/allhallowtide/all-souls-day)
