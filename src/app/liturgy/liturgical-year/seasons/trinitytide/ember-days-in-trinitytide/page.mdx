<Initial text="Ember Days in Trinitytide" />

**Wednesday, Friday, Saturday After Holy Cross**<br/>
*Lesser Observance*

The [Ember Days](/glossary/liturgical-terms#ember-days) in Trinitytide are days of fasting, prayer, and thanksgiving for the fall season. They take place on the Wednesday, Friday, Saturday after the feast of [The Exaltation of the Holy Cross](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/trinitytide/holy-cross). Ember Days teach us to use God's gifts of nature in moderation, and through almsgiving, to assist the needy. Ember Saturday has traditionally been appointed as a day on which ordinations take place.
