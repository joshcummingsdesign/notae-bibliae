**Trinity Sunday – Advent (exclusive)**

Trinitytide (Trinity + -<Definition lang="english" anchor="tide" text="tide" />) is the season encompassing the period of time from Trinity Sunday leading up to [Advent](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/advent).

## Observances

* **First Sunday After Pentecost** — [Trinity Sunday](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/trinitytide/trinity-sunday)
* **Thursday After Trinity Sunday** — [Corpus Christi](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/trinitytide/corpus-christi)
* **July 2** — [Visitation of the Blessed Virgin Mary](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/trinitytide/visitation)
* **August 6** — [Transfiguration of the Lord](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/trinitytide/transfiguration)
* **August 7** — [The Holy Name of Jesus](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/trinitytide/holy-name)
* **September 14** — [Holy Cross Day](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/trinitytide/holy-cross-day)
* **October 31** — [Allhallowtide](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/trinitytide/allhallowtide)
