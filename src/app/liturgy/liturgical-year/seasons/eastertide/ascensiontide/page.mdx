Ascension Day – Pentecost

## Significance

Ascensiontide (Ascension + -<Definition lang="english" anchor="tide" text="tide" />) is the ten-day period between Ascension Day and [Pentecost](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/whitsuntide/pentecost). The Sunday within this period is the Seventh Sunday of Easter.
