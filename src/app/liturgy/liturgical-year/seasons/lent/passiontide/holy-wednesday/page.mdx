<Initial text="Wednesday of Holy Week" />

On Wednesday, the day before the Passover which begins at sundown, Judas Iscariot succumbs to the enemy. He goes to the chief priests and the elders, and they agree to pay him 30 pieces of silver if he delivers Jesus to them. Judas Iscariot then lies in wait for Jesus.
