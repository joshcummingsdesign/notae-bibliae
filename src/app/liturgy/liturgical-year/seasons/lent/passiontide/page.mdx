**Fifth Sunday of Lent – Holy Saturday**

Passiontide (<Definition lang="latin" anchor="passio" text="Passion" /> + -<Definition lang="english" anchor="tide" text="tide" />) is the season encompassing the last two weeks of [Lent](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/lent). It starts with the Fifth Sunday of Lent, which is called Passion Sunday. It goes through Holy Week, and ends on Holy Saturday.

* [Wednesday of Holy Week](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/lent/passiontide/holy-wednesday)
* **Thursday of Holy Week** — [Maundy Thursday](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/lent/passiontide/maundy-thursday)
* **Friday of Holy Week** — [Good Friday](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/lent/passiontide/good-friday)
* **Saturday of Holy Week** — [Holy Saturday](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/lent/passiontide/holy-saturday)
