<Initial text="Ember Days in Lent" />

**Wednesday, Friday, Saturday After the First Sunday of Lent**<br/>
*Lesser Observance*

The [Ember Days](/glossary/liturgical-terms#ember-days) in Lent are days of fasting, prayer, and thanksgiving for the spring season. They take place on the Wednesday, Friday, Saturday after the First Sunday of Lent. Ember Days teach us to use God's gifts of nature in moderation, and through almsgiving, to assist the needy. Ember Saturday has traditionally been appointed as a day on which ordinations take place.
