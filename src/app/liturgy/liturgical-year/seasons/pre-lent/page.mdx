**Septuagesima – Lent (exclusive)**

<Definition lang="english" anchor="lenten" text="Pre-Lent" /> is the season encompassing the period of time from Septuagesima leading up to [Lent](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/lent). It traditionally opens a period of religious instruction leading to the reception of catechumens at Easter. The season focuses on examination of conscience and repentance before the Lenten fast.

## Observances

* **Third Sunday Before Lent** — [Septuagesima](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/pre-lent/septuagesima)
* **Second Sunday Before Lent** — [Sexagesima](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/pre-lent/sexagesima)
* **Sunday Before Lent** — [Quinquagesima](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/pre-lent/quinquagesima)
