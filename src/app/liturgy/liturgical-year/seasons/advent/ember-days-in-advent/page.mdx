<Initial text="Ember Days in Advent" />

**Wednesday, Friday, Saturday After Saint Lucy**<br/>
*Lesser Observance*

The [Ember Days](/glossary/liturgical-terms#ember-days) in Advent are days of fasting, prayer, and thanksgiving for the winter season. They take place on the Wednesday, Friday, Saturday after the commemoration of [St. Lucy](/people/saints/early/lucy-of-syracuse). Ember Days teach us to use God's gifts of nature in moderation, and through almsgiving, to assist the needy. Ember Saturday has traditionally been appointed as a day on which ordinations take place.
