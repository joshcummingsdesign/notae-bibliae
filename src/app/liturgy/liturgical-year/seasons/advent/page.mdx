**Fourth Sunday Before Christmas – Christmastide**

Beginning the liturgical year, <Definition lang="latin" anchor="adventus" text="Advent" /> is the season encompassing the four Sundays leading up to [Christmastide](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/christmastide). Advent is about preparing our hearts and our minds for the coming of Christ. The Nativity Fast was originally 40 days to represent Jesus' 40 days in the wilderness, but it was later shortened to four weeks. Thus, Advent begins on the fourth Sunday before Christmas.
