<Initial text="Ember Days in Whitsuntide" />

**Wednesday, Friday, Saturday After Pentecost**<br/>
*Lesser Observance*

The [Ember Days](/glossary/liturgical-terms#ember-days) in Whitsuntide are days of fasting, prayer, and thanksgiving for the summer season. They take place on the Wednesday, Friday, Saturday after [Pentecost (Whitsunday)](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/whitsuntide/pentecost). Ember Days teach us to use God's gifts of nature in moderation, and through almsgiving, to assist the needy. Ember Saturday has traditionally been appointed as a day on which ordinations take place.
