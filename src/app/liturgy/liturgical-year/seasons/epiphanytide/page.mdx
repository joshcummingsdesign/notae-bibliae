Epiphanytide (<Definition lang="greek" anchor="epiphaneia" text="Epiphany" /> + -<Definition lang="english" anchor="tide" text="tide" />) is the season encompassing the period of time from Epiphany leading up to [Pre-Lent](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/pre-lent).
