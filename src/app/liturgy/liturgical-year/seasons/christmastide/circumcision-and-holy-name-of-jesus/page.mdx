**January 1**<br/>
Eight Days After Christmas<br/>
*Feast*

The feast of the Circumcision and Holy Name of Jesus celebrates the eighth day after Christ was born, when he was circumcised according to the Law (Gen. 17:12-13) (Lev. 12:2-3) and given the Name [Jesus](/glossary/names#jesus) (Luke 2:21), as the angel Gabriel foretold (Matt. 1:21). On this day he first shed his blood, bearing witness to his true humanity and pointing to his saving work upon the cross. Those who call upon his Name shall be saved (Rom. 10:13).
