January 1 (Eight Days After Christmas)

## Significance

Eight days after Christ was born, he was circumcised according to the Law (Gen. 17:12-13) (Lev. 12:2-3), and was given the name Jesus (Luke 2:21), as the angel Gabriel foretold (Matt. 1:21).

## Readings

- Abraham and the Covenant of Circumcision (Gen. 17:9-14)
- The Law of Circumcision (Lev. 12:2-3)
- Jesus' Birth Foretold (Matt. 1:21)
- The Circumcision and Naming of Jesus (Luke 2:21)
