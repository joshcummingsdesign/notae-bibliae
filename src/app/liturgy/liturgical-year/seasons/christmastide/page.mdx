**Christmas Day – Epiphanytide**

Chistmastide (<Definition lang="english" anchor="christmas" text="Christmas" /> + -<Definition lang="english" anchor="tide" text="tide" />) is the season encompassing the 12 days from Christmas Day leading up to [Epiphanytide](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/epiphanytide).

## Observances

* **December 25** — [Christmas Day](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/christmastide/christmas-day)
* **December 26** — [Saint Stephen](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/christmastide/stephen), Deacon and Martyr
* **December 27** — [Saint John](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/christmastide/john), Apostle and Evangelist
* **December 28** – [The Holy Innocents](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/christmastide/holy-innocents)
* **January 1** — [Circumcision and Holy Name of Jesus](/liturgy/liturgical-year/seasons/christmastide/circumcision-and-holy-name-of-jesus)
