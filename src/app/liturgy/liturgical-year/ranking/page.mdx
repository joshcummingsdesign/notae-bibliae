### Precedence

- If a Feast falls on a higher-ranking day, the Feast is transferred to the next available date.
- If a Feast falls on a Sunday, the Feast takes precedence, but the [collect](/glossary/liturgical-terms#collect) for the Sunday is said after the collect for the Feast.
- If a Commemoration falls on a higher-ranking day, the Commemoration is omitted.
- If a Commemoration falls on a Sunday, the collect for the Commemoration is said after the collect for the Sunday.
- Principal Feasts have Vigils that begin at Evening Prayer on the preceding day.
