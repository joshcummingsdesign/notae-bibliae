## Morning Prayer

Nine months before [The Nativity of the Blessed Virgin Mary](/liturgy/liturgical-year/saints/nativity-of-the-blessed-virgin-mary), the feast of the Conception of the Blessed Virgin Mary is celebrated. The feast honors [Mary](/people/saints/biblical/blessed-virgin-mary) as specially chosen and prepared by God to bear Jesus. It highlights her purity, devotion, and obedience from the very beginning of her life. It marks a devotional opportunity to reflect on God's providence and Mary's faithfulness, encouraging the faithful to follow her example of obedience to God. Since it falls during Advent, the feast also naturally anticipates the coming of Christ, linking Mary's conception to the wider theme of God's preparation for the Incarnation.
