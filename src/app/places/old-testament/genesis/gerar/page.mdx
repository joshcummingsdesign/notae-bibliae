- Southeast of [Gaza](/places/old-testament/genesis/gaza) in the territory of the Philistines.

- Abraham settles there, and afraid for his life, tells Abimelech that Sarah is his sister (Gen. 20:1-2).

- Isaac goes there when there is a famine in the land (Gen. 26:1), and God appears to him and affirms that his promise to Abraham will carry on through Isaac (Gen. 26:2-3), and that all the nations of the earth will be blessed in his offspring (Gen. 26:4-5).
