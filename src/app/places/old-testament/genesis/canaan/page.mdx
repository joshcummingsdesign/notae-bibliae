- Its name comes from Canaan, the son of Ham (Gen. 9:18).

- The land that was promised to Abraham and later became Israel (Gen. 10:19) (Gen. 15:18-21).

- Terah takes Abraham, Sarah, and Lot up from [Ur](/places/old-testament/genesis/ur) to go to Canaan, but they settle in [Haran](/places/old-testament/genesis/haran) (Gen. 11:31). Later, Abraham takes Sarah and Lot to Canaan (Gen. 12:5).

- God tells Israel to drive out seven nations from the land of Canaan: Hittites, the Girgashites, the Amorites, the Canaanites, the Perizzites, the Hivites, and the Jebusites (Deut. 7:1-2).
