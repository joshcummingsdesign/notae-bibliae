- Ur was an ancient city in the region of [Chaldea](/places/old-testament/genesis/chaldea) in southern [Babylonia](/places/old-testament/genesis/babylonia).

- Terah takes Abraham, Sarah, and Lot up from Ur to go to [Canaan](/places/old-testament/genesis/canaan), but they settle in [Haran](/places/old-testament/genesis/haran) (Gen. 11:31). Later, Abraham takes Sarah and Lot to Canaan (Gen. 12:5).
