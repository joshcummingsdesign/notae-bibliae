- The land east of the [Sinai Peninsula](/places/old-testament/exodus/mount-sinai) in the modern northwest Arabian Peninsula.

- Named after Abraham and Keturah's son Midian who Abraham sent eastward (Gen. 25:1) (Gen 25:6).

- Moses flees there after striking down an Egyptian (Ex. 2:14-15).

- Jethro, Moses' father-in-law, is the priest of Midian (Ex. 3:1).
