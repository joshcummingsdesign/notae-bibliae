- Assyria was an ancient kingdom northeast of [Babylonia](/places/old-testament/genesis/babylonia) in Upper Mesopotamia (modern northern Iraq and southeastern Turkey) that included the city of [Nineveh](/places/old-testament/genesis/nineveh).

- Its name comes from Asshur, the son of Shem (Gen. 10:22).
