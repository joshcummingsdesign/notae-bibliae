- Engedi, also called Hazazon-tamar (2 Chron. 20:2), is a place on the western shore of the [Dead Sea](/places/old-testament/genesis/dead-sea).

- In the time of Abraham, Babylonian king Chedorlaomer defeats the Amorites who had settled there (Gen. 14:7).
