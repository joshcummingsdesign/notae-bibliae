<Initial text="Abel-mizraim (Atad)"/>

- A place east of the [Jordan](/places/old-testament/genesis/jordan) where Joseph and all his brothers wept for the death of Jacob.

- Abel-mizraim (אָבֵל מִצְרַיִם) means "mourning (or meadow) of Egypt".

- It was formerly known as the Threshing Floor of Atad (Gen. 50:11). Atad (אָטָד) means "thornbush" or "bramble".
