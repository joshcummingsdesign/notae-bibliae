- A desert east of the [Negeb](/places/old-testament/genesis/negeb) and north of the [Sinai Peninsula](/places/old-testament/genesis/mount-sinai) on the way to [Egypt](/places/old-testament/genesis/egypt).

- [Beer-lahai-roi](/places/old-testament/genesis/beer-lahai-roi) lies between [Kadesh](/places/old-testament/genesis/kadesh) and [Shur](/places/old-testament/genesis/shur) (Gen. 16:14).

- Along with [Havilah](/places/old-testament/genesis/havilah) is listed as a boundary of the territory of the Ishmaelites (Gen. 25:17-18).

- After Moses and the Israelites cross the [Red Sea](/places/old-testament/genesis/red-sea), they go into the wilderness of Shur and find no water (Ex. 15:22).
