- South of [Zoar](/places/old-testament/genesis/zoar) and [Admah](/places/old-testament/genesis/admah) near [Sodom](/places/old-testament/genesis/sodom) and [Gomorrah](/places/old-testament/genesis/gomorrah) in the Jordon Valley.

- Shinab king of Admah is one of the five kings that Amraphel king of [Shinar](/places/old-testament/genesis/shinar) ([Babylonia](/places/old-testament/genesis/babylon)) wars with in the time of Abraham (Gen. 14:1). Lot gets captured, and Abraham rescues him with just 318 men (Gen. 14:14-16).

- God destroys Sodom, Gomorrah, Admah, and Zeboiim for their great sin (Gen. 19:23-25) (Deut. 29:23).
