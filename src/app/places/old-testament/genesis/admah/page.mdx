- South of the [Dead Sea](/places/old-testament/genesis/dead-sea) near [Sodom](/places/old-testament/genesis/sodom) and [Gomorrah](/places/old-testament/genesis/gomorrah).

- Shinab king of Admah is one of the five kings that Amraphel king of Shinar ([Babylonia](/places/old-testament/genesis/babylonia)) wars with in the time of Abraham (Gen. 14:1). Lot gets captured, and Abraham rescues him with just 318 men (Gen. 14:14-16).

- God destroys Sodom, Gomorrah, Admah, and [Zeboiim](/places/old-testament/genesis/zeboiim) for their great sin (Gen. 19:23-25) (Deut. 29:23).
