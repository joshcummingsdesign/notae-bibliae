- Paran, or El-paran, is a wilderness region in the Sinai Peninsula, north of [Mount Sinai](/places/old-testament/exodus/mount-sinai).

- Listed as a boundary of the land of the Horites (Gen. 14:6).

- Ishmael dwelt there after he was sent away by Abraham (Gen. 21:21).
