- Kadesh-barnea, also simply called Kadesh, is a place in the desert in the [Negeb](/places/old-testament/genesis/negeb) in the [Wilderness of Zin](/places/old-testament/numbers/wilderness-of-zin) in what became southern Judah.

- In the time of Abraham, Babylonian king Chedorlaomer defeats the Amalekites in Kadesh-barnea (Gen. 14:5-7).

- [Beer-lahai-roi](/places/old-testament/genesis/beer-lahai-roi) lies between Kadesh-barnea and [Shur](/places/old-testament/genesis/shur) (Gen 16:14).
