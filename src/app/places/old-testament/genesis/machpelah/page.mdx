- Abraham buys a burial site from Ephron the Hittite in [Hebron](/places/old-testament/genesis/hebron) just east of the [Oaks of Mamre](/places/old-testament/genesis/oaks-of-mamre) at Machpelah (Gen. 23:7-9).
  - Sarah is buried there by Abraham (Gen. 23:19).
  - Abraham is buried there with Sarah by Ishmael and Isaac (Gen. 25:8-10).
  - Isaac is buried there by Esau and Jacob (Gen. 35:27-29).
  - Rebekah is buried there (Gen. 49:31-32).
  - Leah is buried there by Jacob (Gen. 49:31-32).
  - Jacob is buried there by Joseph and all his brothers (Gen. 50:13-14).
