<Initial text="Nod"/>

- The place where Cain settles after being sent away from the Lord (Gen. 4:16).

- The word Nod in Hebrew (נוֹד) means "wandering".

- It is to the east of [Eden](/places/old-testament/genesis/eden), and like Eden its location is a mystery (Gen. 4:16).
