- The Negeb is a large dry desert land in the south of Israel.

- [Beersheba](/places/old-testament/genesis/beersheba), [Kadesh](/places/old-testament/genesis/kadesh), the [Wilderness of Zin](/places/old-testament/genesis/wilderness-of-zin), and the [Wilderness of Paran](/places/old-testament/genesis/wilderness-of-paran) are all located there.

- After Abraham builds an altar between [Bethel](/places/old-testament/genesis/bethel) and [Ai](/places/old-testament/genesis/ai), he journeys on toward the Negeb (Gen. 12:8-9).

- There Isaac and Rebekah meet for the first time (Gen. 24:62-64).
