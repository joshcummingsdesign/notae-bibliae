- North of the [Red Sea](/places/old-testament/exodus/red-sea), south of [Etham](/places/old-testament/exodus/etham), just east of [Baal-zephon](/places/old-testament/exodus/baal-zephon).

- Moses and the Israelites leave Etham and camp there right before they cross the Red Sea (Ex. 14:1-2).

- Pharaoh's army pursues the Israelites there (Ex. 14:9).
