- On the edge of the wilderness east of [Succoth, Egypt](/places/old-testament/exodus/succoth-egypt) and north of the [Red Sea](/places/old-testament/exodus/red-sea).

- On the Passover when Pharaoh let's Israel go, they go from [Rameses](/places/old-testament/genesis/rameses) to Succoth (Egypt) and then to Etham (Ex. 13:20).

- Here the Lord goes before Moses and the Israelites in a pillar of cloud by day and a pillar of fire by night (Ex. 13:21-22).
