- The Hebrew name Rahab (רָחָב, racháv) means “broad”, “pride”, or “arrogance”. It is used as a way to refer to [Egypt](/places/genesis/egypt) (Job 9:13) (Ps. 87:4), but is also used to refer to Leviathan (Isa. 51:9).
