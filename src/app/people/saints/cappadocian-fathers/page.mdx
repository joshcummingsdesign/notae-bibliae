The Cappadocian Fathers are a 4th-century monastic family from the Cappadocia region of modern Turkey, an early center of Christian activity.

- [Basil the Great](/people/saints/cappadocian-fathers/basil-the-great) (379 AD)
- [Gregory of Nazianzus](/people/saints/cappadocian-fathers/gregory-of-nazianzus) (390 AD)
- [Gregory of Nyssa](/people/saints/cappadocian-fathers/gregory-of-nyssa) (394 AD)
