<Initial text="Desert Fathers" />

The Desert Fathers are Church Fathers who lived in the Egyptian desert in the 4th century and are credited with beginning the practice of monasticism.

- [Paul of Thebes](/people/saints/desert-fathers/paul-of-thebes) (227–341 AD)
- [Anthony the Great](/people/saints/desert-fathers/anthony-the-great) (251–356 AD)
- [Pachomius the Great](/people/saints/desert-fathers/pachomius-the-great) (290–348 AD)
