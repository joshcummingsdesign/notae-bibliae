[Gabriel](/glossary/names#gabriel) is the archangel who explains to [Daniel](/bible/old-testament/prophetic-books/daniel) the vision of the ram and the goat, and who gives Daniel the prophecy of 70 weeks. He also foretells the birth of [St. John the Baptist](/people/saints/biblical/john-the-baptist) and Jesus.

## Readings

- Daniel's Vision of the Ram and the Goat (Dan. 8:15-26)
- The Seventy Weeks (Daniel 9:20-27)
- Birth of John the Baptist Foretold (Luke 1:5-19)
- Birth of Jesus Foretold (Luke 1:26-33)
