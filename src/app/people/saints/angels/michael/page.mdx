[Michael](/glossary/names#michael) is the archangel who fights for the faithful and leads the war in heaven against Satan.

## Readings

- The Ancient of Days Reigns (Dan. 7:9-10)
- The Son of Man Is Given Dominion (Dan. 7:13-14)
- Daniel and the Son of Man (Dan. 10:1-21)
- The Time of the End (Dan. 12:1-13)
- Signs of the End of the Age (Matt. 24:1-31)
- My Refuge and My Fortress (Ps. 91:1-16)
- Satan Thrown Down to Earth (Rev. 12:7-12)
