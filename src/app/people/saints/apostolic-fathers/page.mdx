<Initial text="Apostolic Fathers" />

The Apostolic Fathers are Church Fathers who lived in the 1st and 2nd centuries AD, and were believed to have personally known some of the twelve apostles, or to have been significantly influenced by them.

- [Clement of Rome](/people/saints/apostolic-fathers/clement-of-rome) (99 AD)
- [Ignatius of Antioch](/people/saints/apostolic-fathers/ignatius-of-antioch) (108 AD)
- [Quadratus of Athens](/people/saints/apostolic-fathers/quadratus-of-athens) (129 AD)
- [Papias of Hierapolis](/people/saints/apostolic-fathers/papias-of-hierapolis) (130 AD)
- [Polycarp of Smyrna](/people/saints/apostolic-fathers/polycarp-of-smyrna) (155 AD)
