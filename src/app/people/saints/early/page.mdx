- [Valentine of Rome](/people/saints/early/valentine-of-rome) (d. 269 AD)
- [Nicholas of Myra](/people/saints/early/nicholas-of-myra) (270–343 AD)
- [Epiphanius of Salamis](/people/saints/early/epiphanius-of-salamis) (310–403 AD)
- [John Cassian](/people/saints/early/john-cassian) (360–435 AD)
- [Patrick of Ireland](/people/saints/early/patrick-of-ireland) (386–461 AD)
