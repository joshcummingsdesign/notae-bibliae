**Evangelist** (1st century AD)

[St. Luke](/glossary/names#luke) was a Gentile disciple of Jesus from the Hellenistic city of Antioch in Ancient Syria. He was a physician and a traveling companion of [St. Paul](/people/saints/apostles/paul) (Acts 16:10). He wrote the [Gospel of Luke](/bible/new-testament/gospels/luke).

## Observances

- **October 18** — [Saint Luke](/liturgy/liturgical-year/saints/saint-luke), Evangelist
