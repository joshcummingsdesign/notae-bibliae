<Initial text="Theological Terms" />

## Abomination of Desolation

A type of the Antichrist or the fulfillment thereof.

## Antichrist

A type of adversary against Christ who prefigures, but is not he fulfillment of, the son of perdition who will emerge in the Great Tribulation.

## Beatific Vision

The direct, face-to-face vision of God as he is.

## City of God

The whole communion of saints and angels destined for eternal union with God.

## End of the Age

The time of the Second Coming.

## Final Judgment

The public manifestation of Christ's perfect justice at the End of the Age.

## First Resurrection

Regeneration by the Holy Spirit through Baptism.

## Great Tribulation

The final trial on earth before the Second Coming.

## Heaven

In Scripture, a spatial metaphor for God’s transcendent presence; in theology, the eternal state of blessedness in God’s presence.

## New Heavens and New Earth

The renewed creation in the Age to Come.

## Predestination

God's eternal plan to grant us grace in order to send us to our ultimate end, which is eternal life with him.

## Resurrection of Life

Those who rise to eternal life in the Second Resurrection.

## Resurrection of Judgment

Those who rise to condemnation in the Second Resurrection.

## Second Coming

Jesus' return.

## Second Death

Damnation.

## Second Resurrection

The bodily resurrection at the End of the Age.

## Age to Come

The time after the Second Coming.

## The Antichrist

The son of perdition through whom Satan will work to deceive the nations, and who will exalt himself in the Great Tribulation.

## The Consummation

The time of the Second Coming.

## This Age

The present time, the time before the Second Coming, or the Church age.
