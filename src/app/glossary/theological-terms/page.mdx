<Initial text="Theological Terms" />

## Abomination of Desolation

A type of the Antichrist or the fulfillment thereof.

## Age to Come

The time after the Second Coming.

## An Antichrist

A type of adversary against Christ who prefigures, but is not he fulfillment of, the son of perdition who will emerge in the Great Tribulation.

## Beatific Vision

The <Definition lang="latin" anchor="beatificus" text="Beatific Vision" /> is seeing God face to face as he is in the New Heavens and the New Earth.

## City of God

The whole communion of saints and angels destined for eternal union with God.

## Elect

One exalted by God for a special purpose. The faithful remnant. Those who God knows will endure in faith to the end.

## Election

God's foreknowledge of those who accept his offer of salvation. God's prerogative to exalt an individual for his good purposes.

## End of the Age

The time of the Second Coming.

## Final Judgment

The public manifestation of Christ's perfect justice at the End of the Age.

## First Resurrection

Regeneration by the Holy Spirit through Baptism.

## Great Apostasy

The devil's great deception of the nations leading to a <Definition lang="greek" anchor="apostasia" text="falling away" /> during the Great Tribulation.

## Great Tribulation

The final trial on earth before the Second Coming.

## Heaven

**General**

Where God dwells in glory, where Christ reigns at the right hand of the Father, and where God rules over all creation.

**After Death**

The state of rest in Christ in blessedness until the Second Resurrection and Final Judgment.

**After Resurrection**

Eternal blessedness with God in the New Heavens and New Earth, when the Beatific Vision is realized.

## Hell

**General**

The whole realm of the dead, where all await the Second Resurrection and Final Judgment.

**After Death**

The state of separation from God in torment until the resurrection of the body.

**After Resurrection**

Eternal separation from God after the Final Judgment, also called the Second Death or damnation.

## New Heavens and New Earth

The renewed creation in the Age to Come.

## Predestination

God's eternal plan to grant us grace in order to send us to our ultimate end, which is eternal life with him.

## Resurrection of Life

Those who rise to eternal life in the Second Resurrection.

## Resurrection of Judgment

Those who rise to condemnation in the Second Resurrection.

## Second Coming

Jesus' return.

## Second Death

Damnation.

## Second Resurrection

The bodily resurrection at the End of the Age.

## Six Ages of the World

The view that there are six periods in salvation history leading to the End of the Age that correspond to the six days of creation leading to the Sabbath.

1. Adam to Noah
2. Noah to Abraham
3. Abraham to David
4. David to Exile
5. Exile to Christ
6. Christ to Now (This Age)

## The Antichrist

The son of perdition through whom Satan will work to deceive the nations, and who will exalt himself in the Great Tribulation.

## This Age

The present time, the time before the Second Coming, or the Church age.
