## Overview

An analysis of the prophecy of 1,335 days from the book of Daniel. In Daniel's vision, four beasts come up out of the sea. The fourth has ten horns. Daniel prophesies that the <Definition lang="english" anchor="the-antichrist" text="Antichrist" /> will emerge, but God will throw him into a river of fire. Then the Son of Man will be given everlasting dominion, glory, and a kingdom. In the end, the faithful are delivered.
