## Synopsis

[St. John's](/people/saints/john-of-zebedee/) second letter which he wrote to a house church in Ephesus in around 85–95 AD. The church communities in Ephesus had recently gone through a crisis involving a group of people who broke away from the church, denied Jesus, and stirred up hostility among those who remained faithful. John warns the believers not to support these individuals, whom he calls deceivers.
