## Synopsis

[St. Peter's](/people/saints/peter) second letter to the churches in Asia Minor which he wrote while awaiting execution in Rome in around 64–67 AD. In his letter, Peter offers a challenge to followers of Jesus to confirm their calling, followed by two warnings about a growing number of corrupt teachers leading believers astray.
