## Synopsis

[St. Paul's](/people/saints/apostles/paul) final letter and second letter to [St. Timothy](/people/saints/timothy) which he wrote awaiting death during his second imprisonment in Rome in around 64–67 AD. Paul calls Timothy, who is in Ephesus, to come be with Paul in prison so he can pass on to him the mission he started.
