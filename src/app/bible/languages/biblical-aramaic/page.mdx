<Initial text="Biblical Aramaic"/>

Popular Near-Eastern language used originally by the ancient Arameans (modern Syria). Jews began to move toward speaking Aramaic during the Babylonian captivity.

## Passages

* Genesis 31:47
* Proverbs 31:2
* Jeremiah 10:11
* Daniel 2:4 – Daniel 7:28
* Ezra 4:8 – Ezra 6:18 and Ezra 7:12-26
