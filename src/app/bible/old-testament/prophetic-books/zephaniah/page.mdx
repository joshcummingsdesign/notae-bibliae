## Synopsis

The prophecy of <Definition lang="hebrew" anchor="tzefanya" text="Zephaniah" />, descendant of Hezekiah, in the days of Josiah regarding the downfall of Southern Judah. Zephaniah prophesies God's judgment on the nations, and the hope of God's healing and purification of the nations into a unified family in Jerusalem, thus fulfilling God's promise to Abraham.
