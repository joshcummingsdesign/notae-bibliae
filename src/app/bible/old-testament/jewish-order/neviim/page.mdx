The <Definition lang="hebrew" anchor="neviim" text="Nevi’im"/> consist of 8 books divided into two parts: the Former Prophets and the Latter Prophets. The Latter Prophets include the three major prophets, and one book consisting of the the twelve minor prophets.

## Former Prophets (4)

* [Joshua](/bible/old-testament/historical-books/joshua)
* [Judges](/bible/old-testament/historical-books/judges)
* [1 Samuel](/bible/old-testament/historical-books/1-samuel) - [2 Samuel](/bible/old-testament/historical-books/2-samuel)
* [1 Kings](/bible/old-testament/historical-books/1-kings) - [2 Kings](/bible/old-testament/historical-books/2-kings)
