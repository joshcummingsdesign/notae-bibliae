<Initial text="The Living Bible"/>

An English paraphrase of the [American Standard Version](/bible/translations/american-standard-version) by American publisher Kenneth N. Taylor, which was published in 1971. Taylor was inspired by his children, and wanted a Bible that they could easily understand. There is also a Catholic edition.

* English
