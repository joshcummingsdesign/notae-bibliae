<Initial text="New Revised Standard Version"/>

Published in 1989, the National Council of the Churches (NCC), an ecumenical partnership of 38 Christian faith groups in the United States, set off to update the [Revised Standard Version](/bible/translations/revised-standard-version). There is also a Catholic edition which was approved by the United States Conference of Catholic Bishops (USCCB).
